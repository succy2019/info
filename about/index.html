My Trading Methods

My processes are simple and effective, her process spans through three stages which makes it transparent and worry-free.

Look Down Before You Look Up

All great traders determine what they are willing to lose before they think about what they will earn on a trade. Decide before your enter a trade where you will exit.

Remember your opinion doesn't matter only the markets moves!

Let the market determine what direction its going to move in and not your feeling! Discipled traders respond intelligently and wisely to price action.

Trade your personality, account size & style!

Identity your ideal timeframe, trade size and financial goals. Avoid being influenced by trading profit generated by other Traders. Trading is an individual performance business, and profitable traders trade their accounts without being swayed by FOMO.
