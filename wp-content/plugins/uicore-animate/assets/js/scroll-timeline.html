Do I need a financial advisor?

If you need help managing money or planning for the future—yes.

How often should I meet with my advisor?

At least once per quarter, or as needed.

Can a financial advisor help with debt?

Yes—many advisors create debt-management strategies.

Credentials, experience, transparency and compatibility.
